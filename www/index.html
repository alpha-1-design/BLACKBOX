Privacy Policy

Our Core Promise

ShieldSpace was built on one principle: your data belongs to you, only you. We do not collect, transmit, store or sell any of your personal information.

What Stays On Your Device

  • All vault notes and files — encrypted locally with AES-256-GCM
  • Your PIN — never stored in plaintext, used only for key derivation
  • Intruder photos — encrypted and stored only on your device
  • App settings and preferences
  • Browsing session data — wiped automatically on exit

Network Usage

ShieldSpace only uses the internet for one thing: the secure mini-browser feature, when you choose to visit a URL. No app data, no analytics, no crash reports are ever sent anywhere.

Camera Permission

Camera access is used only for the Intruder Selfie feature and Camera Watch. Photos taken are stored encrypted on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be included in the app update. Last updated: March 2026.

🔑 Forgot your PIN?

ShieldSpace cannot recover your PIN — this is by design. Your encryption key is derived from your PIN, so resetting it means losing vault data. If you're locked out, go to Settings → Factory Reset to start fresh.

🛡 Overlay not working?

Go to your phone Settings → search "Display over other apps" → find ShieldSpace → toggle ON. On some phones this is under Special App Access or Privacy Protection.

👆 Fingerprint not working?

Make sure you have a fingerprint enrolled in your phone's Settings → Security → Fingerprint. Then open ShieldSpace and tap the fingerprint button on the lock screen.

Email us at alphariansamuel@gmail.com with your phone model and what happened. We respond within 48 hours.
